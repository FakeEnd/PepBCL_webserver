Instructions

To use the classifier to predict protein-peptide binding residues from sequence, either:

  • paste your FASTA-formatted protein sequences into the text area, or
  • upload a FASTA-formatted file with your protein sequences.

Fasta must be the same format like sample

Press the "Submit" button to upload the sequences and begin the classification.

Waiting a moment and your results will be presented in web page.
